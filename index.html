WELCOME TO NORTH EAST ENGINEERS

NEE provides Civil Laboratory and Consultancy Services

North East Engineers was founded in 1992 under the Directorship of Mr. C. K. Bezbaruah, in collaboration with a group of engineers from various disciplines, each with expertise in their respective fields

The consultant's objective is to disseminate the knowledge of modern technologies from industrialized countries, implement modernized residential structural colonies, and establish smooth road networks in developing nations.

SPECIALIST IN

Geotechnical Investigation, All Type of Pile Load Test, Pile Integrity Test, Block Vibration Test (BVT),Staticc Cone Penetration (SCPT), DCPT, Cube Testing, Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV), NDT Test, Rock Core Drilling, Architectural Drawing & Structural Design, Civil Construction & Project Monitoring and Land Survey (Topographical)
